1 · Three-register number system

Net worth = ordinary (text color). Wealth Score = answer (accent — teal/indigo). +£8.2K · 7d on Today card = diff (gold). Three discrete affordances, all on the same anchor row.

2 · viewMode quieter register

Future card: text drops from weight 800 → 700, color shifts to --c-text-projected (~20% desaturated), polygon stroke goes dashed, fill drops to 7% alpha, glow removed. Mode pill ("Future · 2031") is the backup label. Both signals together; neither alone.

3 · Bounded glass

Glass appears in exactly one place: the radial blur backdrop behind the polygon (.sw-radar-glass). Card chrome is solid gradient. Numbers sit on opaque background. No glass behind anything that needs to be read.

4 · Dim identity vs theme

The 7 node dots wear their locked identity colors (blue Habits, teal Own, amber Tax, red Safety, green Flow, purple Debt, orange Legacy). The aggregate polygon wears the theme accent (Mathematical Teal in dark; Action Indigo in light). Two systems, clean separation.

5 · Drag-handle affordance

Each node has a dashed concentric ring (.sw-radar__handle) — the visible cue that the dot is draggable. Today card only; on Future the dots are static (projected, not interactive).

6 · Calm frame, sharp numbers

Card chrome: subtle gradient + hairline border + inset highlight. Quiet. The 28–44px hero numbers and accented Wealth Score carry all the visual weight. Two registers in one card.
