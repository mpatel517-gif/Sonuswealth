# Cookie Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-05-28

We use a small number of cookies and similar technologies. This page lists each one, why we use it, and how to control it.

## What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. Similar technologies include `localStorage`, `sessionStorage`, and IndexedDB. We use the term "cookies" for all of these.

## Cookies we use

### Strictly necessary (no consent required)

These are required for the Service to function. Disabling them will break basic functionality.

| Name | Purpose | Storage | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| `sb-access-token` | Authentication session | Cookie / localStorage | Session + 7-day refresh |
| `sb-refresh-token` | Refresh authentication | Cookie / localStorage | 7 days |
| `sw_theme` | Your light/dark preference | localStorage | Until cleared |
| `sonus:taxyear` | Your selected tax year | localStorage | Until cleared |
| `sonuswealth.temporal` | Cross-tab tax-year sync | localStorage | Until cleared |

### Analytics (optional — consent required)

When enabled, these help us understand how the Service is used so we can improve it.

| Name | Purpose | Storage | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| `ph_*` | PostHog product analytics (page views, named events; **no autocapture, no session recording, no £ amounts**) | Cookie + localStorage | 1 year |

Analytics cookies are **off by default**. They are only set if you click "Accept analytics" on the cookie banner. You can change your choice at any time in Settings → Privacy.

### Error reporting (optional — consent required)

| Name | Purpose | Storage | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| `sentry-*` | Error monitoring (Sentry) — captures exceptions and stack traces. No financial data sent. | Cookie / memory | Session |

Off by default. Activated on consent.

## Cookies we do **not** use

- No advertising cookies
- No tracking pixels
- No third-party social media cookies
- No cross-site behavioural profiling

## Your choices

- **Cookie banner** — shown on first visit. Choose "Strictly necessary only" or "Accept analytics".
- **Settings → Privacy** — change your choices at any time.
- **Browser controls** — block or delete cookies in your browser settings. Note: blocking strictly-necessary cookies will sign you out and reset preferences.

## Do Not Track

We respect the Do Not Track signal. When DNT is on, analytics cookies are not set even if you previously consented.
