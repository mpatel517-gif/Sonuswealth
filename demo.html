90% certainty
You hold £380,000 in your General Investment Account (GIA), exposed to income tax on dividends and CGT on gains. Your ISA has £12,000 unused allowance this year. Sell £12,000 from your GIA — using your £3,000 CGT Annual Exempt Amount to minimise CGT — and immediately re-buy the same holdings inside your ISA. Action steps

Sell £12,000 worth of these GIA holdings
Immediately re-buy the same holdings inside your ISA (most platforms automate this as "Bed and ISA")
Complete before April 5
Context
