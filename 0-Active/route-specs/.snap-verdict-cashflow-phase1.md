## Founder decisions taken (this phase)
1. **Surplus is net-of-tax** (was gross-only, hid tax). New canonical `cashflowFlow(entity,bundle)` = gross − income tax − employee NI − pension/ISA contributions − essentials − debt − protection. `monthlySurplus` delegates to it (Home + cashflowHealth align).

- Sankey + waterfall read absent keys (`gross_annual` / `tax_total_annual`) → £0 tax. Now both read `cashflowFlow`.
- Deficit card fired on `surplus<=0` (clamped) → now `ms.deficit>0`.
- Liquidity "5.7 / 6 / 7" = formatting drift (`format="score"` rounding) → one 1-decimal figure everywhere.
- Bill calendar removed from Cashflow (→ Timeline, founder direction).
- PoS curve `terminal*Math.pow(t,1.2)` honestly captioned ("modelled range at year N; intermediate interpolated"); full per-year engine percentiles deferred to Phase 3 §B rebuild.
