What this is, and what changed from the radar you knew.

  • Radar → ConstellationThe 7 dims are now stars sized by score. No polygon fill. The polygon edge is a fine brass thread at 55% opacity — the shape still reads, but it's recessive.
  • Center is emptyNo score in the center anymore. The score moves to its own ticker below, so the chart is for shape, the ticker is for figure. Two facts, two places.
  • Safety net starThe lowest-score dim wears a fine lacquer-red dashed ring — it's the one your brief paragraph names. Chart and prose agree.
  • Hero number above the chartNet worth £487,200 in 88px serif. The big figure is the headline; the chart is the supporting evidence beneath.
  • Brief paragraph mid-cardReal plain-English prose with key figures in brass italic serif. This is the Warren "Your Goal" move adapted — paragraph, not bullets.
  • Three tickersWealth Score (brass — answer register), Risk, Plan progress. Each with a short sub-line. Replaces the three-anchor row I had before.
  • Date stamp + "No. 14 monthly"It IS a standing report. Not a live dashboard refreshing every second. A monthly dispatch you read like a letter.
  • Signoff line"From the Sonuswealth desk · Compiled for Dima — 13 May" — frames this as a document prepared FOR the user, not generated BY a SaaS.
