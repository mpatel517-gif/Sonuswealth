Theme directions — probe 02

v01 was rejected on two grounds: indigo+teal palette didn't carry Sonuswealth's identity, and the "sharp numbers" register was screaming. Both feedbacks point the same way: less volume, more intentionality, real typographic gravity.

Two directions follow. Each has a clear point of view. The radar card content is identical across all four — only the aesthetic moves.

E
Direction · type-pair Fraunces + Manrope

Bedside Lantern

Pulls from Sonu and your own scene quote — phone in a dim room, the app is the only light source. The theme is that lamp: warm parchment text on dusk-blue ground, honey amber on the one number that matters. Light mode reads as vellum.

How they differ where it counts

Dimension
E · Bedside Lantern
B · Annual Report
Identity hook
Sonu the owl reading by lamplight. The product's own backstory.
180 years of finance documents — FT, Economist, ledger books — without cloning any.
Accent color
Honey amber on dusk-blue. Light: terracotta on vellum.
Economist red on charcoal. Light: deep red on cream paper.
Display type
Fraunces — variable serif, SOFT axis dialed warm, characterful curves.
Newsreader — editorial serif, designed for sustained reading, has presence.
Body type
Manrope — geometric humanist, warmer than Inter.
Geist — Swiss-precise, Vercel's open-source ascetic alternative.
Mono
JetBrains Mono — distinctive numerals.
Geist Mono — matches body.
Mood
Quiet · warm · considered · domestic.
Decisive · premium · editorial · professional.
Risk if wrong
Could read "twee" if dialed too soft. Mitigated by serif's gravitas + warm amber's saturation.
Could read "stuffy / old-economy" if dialed too dense. Mitigated by Geist's modern body + restrained color density.
Best for
Consumer-first read. Bedside check-in. Warmth wins novice users.
IFA-first read. Desk session. Decisiveness wins power users.
